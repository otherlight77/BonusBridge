THE CROSS-BORDER GAP

Your insurance history should not
disappear at the border.

A strong driving record does not automatically travel with you. BonusBridge structures the questions, evidence and recognition scenarios to investigate before requesting cover.

⇄

Recognition is not automatic

A destination insurer may use a different rating system or require manual underwriting.

▤

Evidence varies by insurer

Letters, policy certificates, claims records and issue dates may all be assessed differently.

◷

Only part may be recognized

An insurer may cap the accepted years or convert them into a local rating class.

↗

Missing proof can cost more

Without acceptable evidence, a driver may be assessed as having limited local history.
